# Create your first Note in Itomori

Welcome to Itomori. To create your first Note, **follow these easy and simple steps:**

1. Open Itomori App
2. Press your keyboard's `Tab` key to go to the **Note Input** field. You can also use your Mouse.
3. Write your note
4. Press `Enter` key from your keyboard

**Done! Congratulations you created your first note using Itomori.
